Getting Started

System Overview

The Campus Lost and Found System helps students, faculty, and staff report lost items, find items they've lost, and return found items to their rightful owners. The system uses behavioral analytics to identify patterns and improve recovery rates.

Account Access

  • Use your official university email address to log in
  • Only enrolled students, faculty, and staff can access the system
  • Your identity is verified through the university authentication system
  • All activities are logged for security and accountability

Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your personal dashboard with:

  • Quick Actions: Fast access to report lost/found items
  • Statistics: Your personal activity summary
  • Recent Activity: Updates on your reports and claims
  • Tips: Helpful advice for better success rates

Reporting Lost Items

When to Report

  • Report items as soon as you realize they're missing
  • The sooner you report, the higher the chance of recovery
  • Even if you think the item might be at home, it's worth reporting

Required Information

Required Fields

All fields marked with (*) must be completed

  • Item Name: Be specific (e.g., "iPhone 13 Pro Max" not just "phone")
  • Category: Select the most appropriate category
  • Description: Include color, brand, size, unique features
  • Last Seen Location: Where you last remember having it
  • Date & Time: When you last had the item

Tips for Better Results

Include Photos

Upload photos if you have them - increases recovery by 60%

Be Specific

Exact locations and detailed descriptions help

Reporting Found Items

Why Report Found Items?

By reporting found items, you help fellow students and staff recover their belongings. Your honest effort makes the campus community stronger and more trustworthy.

What to Include

  • Photos are required: Help verify ownership
  • Found location: Exactly where you found it
  • Current status: Where the item is now
  • Detailed description: All visible features
  • Date and time found: When you discovered it

Item Security Options

Keep with you

You maintain possession until the owner is verified

Turn in to security

Campus security or lost & found office holds the item

Claiming Items

Verification Process

To claim an item, you must provide proof of ownership. This protects everyone and ensures items are returned to their rightful owners.

1

Describe unique features

Details only the owner would know (scratches, contents, serial numbers)

2

Provide loss details

When and where you lost the item

3

Upload supporting documents

Receipts, photos, or other proof of ownership (optional but helpful)

What Happens Next?

  1. Campus staff review your claim within 24-48 hours
  2. They may contact you for additional verification
  3. If approved, you'll be contacted to arrange pickup
  4. Bring your student ID when collecting the item

Important

False claims may result in disciplinary action. Only claim items that genuinely belong to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do items stay in the system?

Items remain active for 90 days. After that, unclaimed items may be donated to charity or disposed of according to university policy.

Can I edit my report after submitting?

Yes, you can update your reports through the "My Reports" section. However, claims cannot be edited once submitted.

What if I find my lost item after reporting?

Please update your report status to "Found" in the "My Reports" section. This helps keep the system accurate and saves time for staff.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, your contact information is only shared with verified campus personnel and proven item owners. All system activity is logged for security.

Can visitors or guests use the system?

No, only enrolled students, faculty, and staff with valid university credentials can access the system.

What if someone falsely claims my found item?

All claims require verification. Campus staff carefully review proof of ownership before approving any claim. False claims result in disciplinary action.
